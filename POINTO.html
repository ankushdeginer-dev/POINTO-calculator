About POINTO

POINTO is a single-file multi-tool app that includes a scientific calculator, age, tax, currency, unit, BMI, EMI, physics formulas, math helpers, and a cross-tool history system.

  • One file (HTML+CSS+JS).
  • Dark/Light mode, keyboard & voice input, copy & download history.
  • Designed for students and everyday quick calculations.
